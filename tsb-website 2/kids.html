About the Author
A dermatologist who turned education into a story.

Dr. Lisa Prussick is a board-certified dermatologist specializing in inflammatory skin conditions and biologics. She created The Skin Brief: Kids because skin conditions affect millions of children — but few kids have the words to describe what they're feeling. Each book in the series is written to help every child feel seen, understood, and confident in their own skin.
