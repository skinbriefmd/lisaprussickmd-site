Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Lisa Prussick, MD, doing business as The Skin Brief, takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit lisaprussickmd.com and use any tools or content hosted on it. By using the Site, you agree to this policy.

1. What this policy covers

This policy applies to all personal information we collect through the Site, including our tools, blog content, quizzes, and email communications. It does not apply to third-party websites we may link to.

2. Information we collect

We collect information in two ways: directly from you, and automatically through your use of the Site.

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address, when you submit it through a tool on the Site in exchange for access to features or content.
  • Skincare product names or routine information you type into our tools. This is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers after your session ends.
  • Any information you provide when contacting us through the Site's contact form.

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Usage data including pages visited and how you interact with our tools.
  • General location data based on your IP address.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies as described below.

3. Cookies and tracking technologies

Our Site uses cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve your experience. These include standard cookies placed by our hosting provider, Netlify, as part of normal site operation, and basic analytics to understand traffic patterns. We do not currently use advertising cookies or run behavioral advertising campaigns. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

4. How we use your information

  • To provide access to tools and features on the Site.
  • To generate your skincare routine results in real time.
  • To send educational communications you have opted into.
  • To respond to questions or messages submitted through our contact form.
  • To improve the performance and content of the Site.
  • To compile aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. How we share your information

We share your information only with service providers that help us operate the Site:

  • Netlify — our hosting provider, which may collect basic technical data as part of site operation.
  • Kit (formerly ConvertKit) — our email list management platform, which stores email addresses you submit.
  • Anthropic Claude API — the AI service that powers certain tool functionality. Inputs you provide may be processed by this service to generate results.

Each provider has their own privacy policy. We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety.

6. Email communications

If you provide your email address, you may receive educational emails from The Skin Brief. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. You can also request removal by contacting us at hello@lisaprussickmd.com.

7. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Email addresses are stored securely through Kit. Skincare routine information is processed in real time and not retained after your session ends. No internet transmission is 100% secure.

8. Medical information disclaimer

Tool outputs are for educational purposes only and do not constitute medical advice. We are not a healthcare provider and do not create a physician-patient relationship through this Site.

9. Pregnancy and breastfeeding

Tool outputs are not appropriate for use during pregnancy or while breastfeeding without consulting your OB-GYN or a board-certified dermatologist first.

10. Affiliate links

Some links on this Site may be affiliate links. We may earn a small commission if you purchase through them at no additional cost to you. All affiliate relationships are disclosed at the point of use in accordance with FTC guidelines.

11. Your rights

You may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information, and to withdraw consent or opt out of email communications at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@lisaprussickmd.com.

12. Children

The Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

13. Third-party links

The Site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
